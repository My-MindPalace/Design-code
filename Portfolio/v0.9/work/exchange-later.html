Flipkart · Shipped · 2023

Exchange Later

A deferred-time exchange feature letting customers trade their old device after receiving the new one — built for convenience-first users.

Stakeholders Product, Business, Cross-functional teams & Engineering
Contribution Design, Usability Testing & Storyboarding
Org Flipkart

Project Overview

A comprehensive redesign of Flipkart's product exchange process — introducing a deferred-time exchange feature that lets customers trade older devices later, after receiving their new purchase. The initiative targeted convenience-focused users with adoption rate as the primary success metric.

~20% increase in adoption (12% → 32%)
75% growth in devices traded (Dec 2022 – May 2023)
Mobile + Laptop categories impacted

Outcome

A/B testing demonstrated clear improvement over the previous exchange experience. Full-scale rollout achieved approximately 20% increase in adoption. Success was attributed to visual improvements and the convenience factor of deferred exchange — giving users flexibility without friction.
